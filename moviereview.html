Ogniem i Mieczem (1999) is a Polish movie based on a novel of the same name by Henryk Sienkiewicz. Both the book and movie are set in the 17th century Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth during the Khmelnytsky Uprising of 1648, the start of an time in Polish history known as the Deluge, a time of disaster marked by internal rebellion and foreign invasion. The main character is a nobleman by the name of Jan Skrzetuski who promised to marry a noblewoman who was originally betrothed to a cossack noble.
