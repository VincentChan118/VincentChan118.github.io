Future Career: Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a professional engineering career that deals with the physical and natural environment. Civil Engineering includes the design, construction and maintenance of structures such as buildings, roads, and brdiges.

Plan to get into Career

  1. Get into college.
  2. Get into ABET accredited civil engineering program.
  3. Get Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering degree.
  4. Begin working to get professional experiance.
  5. Take Fundamentals of Engineering Exam.
  6. Become licensed engineer.
